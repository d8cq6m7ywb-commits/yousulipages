What you get 测什么

Three measurements. One coach who reads them with you. A clear plan for what to do with the numbers.

三项测试, 由教练亲自解读，并给出可执行的具体建议。

Test 01 · 测试一

RMR, Resting metabolic rate 基础代谢率

How many calories your body burns at rest, measured directly from breath gas (not estimated from a formula). The single most useful number for weight loss, fueling, and screening for under-eating (RED-S).

通过呼气分析直接测量您的基础代谢率 (BMR/RMR)，比公式估算精确得多。是减重、补给和筛查能量不足 (RED-S) 的关键数据。

Why it matters: most diet apps use formulas that miss your real number by 200–500 kcal. Walking out with the actual number changes how you eat and how you train.

大多数手机 App 使用的公式可能与您的真实数值相差 200–500 大卡。带走真实数值，能改变饮食与训练方式。

Test 02 · 测试二

VO2max 最大摄氧量

The single best predictor of fitness and longevity. Measured from breath gas during a graded test (treadmill or stationary bike). High VO2max correlates with longer healthspan, lower all-cause mortality, and better quality of life as you age.

VO2max 是健康与长寿最重要的指标之一。本测试通过逐级递增的运动 (跑步机或自行车) 中的呼气分析直接测量。数值越高，预期寿命越长，年长后生活质量越好。

Used by athletes for training zones; used by everyone else as a vital sign. We’ll plot you against your age & sex norms so the number means something concrete.

运动员用它确定训练区间，普通人则将其视为关键体检指标。我们会按您的年龄与性别给出对照，让数字真正有意义。

Test 03 · 测试三

Body composition 身体成分分析

Lean muscle mass, fat mass, body fat percentage, segmental breakdown (left/right, arms/legs/trunk). Repeat over time to see whether weight changes are coming from fat or from muscle, the scale alone can’t tell you.

瘦体重、脂肪量、体脂百分比、四肢/躯干分段数据。多次测量可以看出体重的变化究竟来自脂肪还是肌肉, 普通体重秤无法分辨。

All three together give a real picture: how much you burn, how fit you are, and what you’re built of.
三项合在一起，呈现完整画面：燃烧多少、心肺多强、身体由什么构成。

How it works 流程

From booking to walking out with your report, about 60 minutes on the day.

01

Get in touch 联系预约

Email info@yousuli.co or message Fred on WeChat (iamea99). Tell us a couple of dates that work and any goals or context. We’ll confirm a time and send the Walnut address + a short pre-test checklist.

02

The day of 测试当天

Come in fasted (overnight, no caffeine). Body comp first, then RMR (15–20 min lying down with a mask), then short break, then VO2max test (8–15 min progressive effort). Bring water + workout clothes.

空腹前来 (前一晚不进食、不喝咖啡)。先做体成分测试，然后是 RMR (戴面罩静卧 15–20 分钟)，短暂休息后进行 VO2max 测试 (递增运动 8–15 分钟)。请携带饮用水与运动服装。

03

Read the numbers together 一起解读数据

Coach Fred walks you through every number. What it means for you specifically, nutrition, training zones, longevity benchmarks. Question time built in. Bilingual where helpful (English / 中文).

Fred 教练逐项讲解。结合您的具体情况说明, 饮食建议、训练区间、长寿对照。设有问答时间。中英文皆可。

04

Take the report home 带走报告

PDF report in your inbox by the next day, plus a one-page summary you can put on the fridge. Email Fred any time afterwards if numbers don’t make sense or anything changes.

次日 PDF 报告发送至邮箱，另有一页可贴冰箱的简明摘要。后续如有疑问随时邮件联系。

FAQ 常见问题

Why is this discounted vs. the regular Lab? 为什么比正常实验室便宜？

Because you’re a neighbour. No commercial overhead, no travel, no marketing, just walking-distance testing for the people in our community. The equipment and the protocol are identical to what we use with elite athletes.

因为是邻居。没有商业附加成本，无需出差，无需推广, 只是为社区里的人提供步行可达的测试。设备与流程与高水平运动员所用完全相同。

I don’t exercise. Will the VO2max test be too hard? 我不运动，VO2max 测试会不会太累？

The test is graded, we start very easy and only increase intensity if you can. We stop at the point that’s right for you. We’ve tested neighbours from age 14 to age 80+, including non-athletes; nobody has had a problem.

测试为渐进式, 起始非常轻松，只在您能承受时才加大强度，并在合适的点停止。从 14 岁到 80 多岁的邻居都做过 (包括完全不运动的人)，无一人出现问题。

Can I bring my parents / spouse / kids on the same day? 能和父母/伴侣/孩子同一天测试吗？

Yes, and most neighbours do. Pick a slot for each person back-to-back. Family rate available, ask when booking.

可以，大部分邻居都会带家人一起。请按家人数量分别预约。提供家庭优惠, 预约时请告知。

Do you speak Chinese? 能用中文交流吗？

The session can be done bilingually. The reports are in English; key sections will be summarised verbally in 中文 if that’s easier. We’ve done this with most of the neighbours in the photo grid above.

可以中英文双语进行。报告以英文为主，但关键部分会用中文口头解读。上方照片中的大部分邻居都是这样完成测试的。

Is this medical advice? 这是医疗建议吗？

No. RMR, VO2max, and body composition are physiological measurements; we read them in a fitness / longevity / nutrition context. If something looks concerning we’ll suggest you see your physician, we don’t replace medical care.

不是。RMR、VO2max、体成分均为生理测量，我们仅在运动、长寿与营养层面解读。若发现异常，会建议就医, 我们不替代医疗。

What if I’m not a Walnut resident? 不是胡桃市居民可以做吗？

This rate is reserved for Walnut neighbours and their immediate family. If you’re elsewhere in greater LA, the regular Performance Lab covers the same testing, and the mobile lab travels.

本优惠仅限胡桃市居民及其直系家属。其他洛杉矶地区可使用正常的 Performance Lab 服务 (移动实验室可上门)。
