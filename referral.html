Yousuli Referral Program.

When you bring another athlete in, both of you get a service credit

If you’ve enjoyed your coaching and know another athlete who’d be a fit for Yousuli, here’s how to make the introduction worth it for both of you. You bring someone in, they sign up and stick with their minimum contract, and a service credit lands on both of your accounts.

How it works ↓ Copy a tell-a-friend message

How it works

Three steps. The first is on you, the next two happen on their own.

1

Tell a friend

Use the copy-paste message below, or just send them a link to yousuli.co/coaching.

2

They sign up and name you

On the intake form when they sign up. If they forget, they have 14 days to add you via the self-service link or by emailing me at info@yousuli.co.

3

Both of you get a credit

Once they complete their minimum (6 months for Bespoke, 3 months for Structured), the service credit lands on both accounts.

The reward

Same amount for both of you, in service credit you can apply to coaching, lab testing, or the Yousuli hub.

New client signs into… Both of you get
Bespoke · $350/mo · 6-month minimum $300 service credit each
Structured · $200/mo · 3-month minimum $100 service credit each

Credit is spendable on

  • Next month of coaching
  • A Performance Lab test (RMR, VO2max, CdA, PRP, full-stack)
  • Yousuli Hub access

Fine print

The actual terms, in plain English.

Yousuli Referral Program · Terms

  • One reward per unique referee.
  • Referrer must be a current Bespoke or Structured client at the time of the referee’s signup.
  • Reward fires at completion of the new client’s contract minimum (6 months Bespoke, 3 months Structured).
  • If the new client cancels with a refund before the trigger date, the reward is revoked.
  • 14-day window for the referee to name a referrer post-signup via the self-service form. No retroactive claims beyond that.
  • Credits have no cash value and are non-transferable.
  • Yousuli reserves the right to investigate or decline rewards in suspected-gaming cases.
  • Questions: fred@yousuli.co.
