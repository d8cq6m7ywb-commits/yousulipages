What to include in your message

The more concrete your message, the faster a useful reply. Doesn’t need to be polished — just specific.

For coaching inquiries

  • Goal & timeline (e.g. sub-3 marathon Boston 2026)
  • Current weekly training hours
  • Recent races / PRs / setbacks
  • Constraints (injury history, schedule, equipment)

For lab bookings

  • Which test(s) you want
  • Your location (we travel within LA + race-week)
  • Time window (next 2–6 weeks ideal)
  • Goal context: race date, training block

For partner / B2B

  • Your org + role
  • Format you have in mind
  • Typical athlete count + cadence
  • Geographic scope

For calculator bugs

  • Which calculator (page name)
  • What you expected vs. what you got
  • Browser + device if it’s a UI issue
  • Screenshot helps but optional
