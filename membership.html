FAQ

Common questions before picking a plan.

Do I need to join the Club to use the Lab?

No. The Performance Lab is open to non-members. Club and coaching athletes get member pricing and priority booking, but anyone can book testing directly.

Can I be in the Club without coaching?

Yes. Many athletes start with Club membership only and add coaching or testing later when the timing makes sense.

Can I upgrade from Club to Coaching?

Yes. You can move into Structured, Bespoke, or Full Performance Coaching at any time, subject to available coaching slots. Roster stays small on purpose.

Do you coach beginners?

Yes. The Club and Basic Foundation package are especially suitable for beginners and returning athletes. Coaching tiers are matched to your current level and goals.

Do you coach only triathletes?

No. We work across triathlon, running, cycling, and swimming. Run-only and bike-only blocks are common. The Lab and calculator library apply across endurance sports.

Discounts for para-athletes & elites?

Yes — the Supported Athletes program reserves limited reduced-rate spots for committed para-athletes and elite athletes targeting national or international performance.
