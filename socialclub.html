Shared epic projects

Pick the one that fits your level. Or invent your own.

Every year, the club picks a few shared challenges that members can opt into — from first-time goals to outright epics. Members train toward them together, encourage each other in the chat, and celebrate finishes (or honest DNFs) the same way.

First-time projects

First 5 km, first 70.3, first open-water mile, first century ride. The biggest leap for the most athletes.

Mid-range goals

Boston-qualifier marathons, IM 70.3 PRs, swim crossings, gravel century blocks.

Long projects

Kona qualifications, mountain crossings, multi-day endurance projects, the kind of thing you train for over a year.
