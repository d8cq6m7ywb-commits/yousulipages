How we make athletes faster

Lab-grade testing, real-world aero, technique work, and a community that doesn’t make beginners feel small.

01

Metabolic & VO₂ testing

Real training zones from real gas exchange. RMR for fueling and RED-S screening. Lab-grade accuracy in a portable mobile setup.

02

Aero & equipment optimization

Field-based CdA testing for free speed. Position, helmet, tires, hydration setup — the changes that save 10–50 watts on race day.

03

Technique & movement

Stroke mechanics, gait analysis, biomechanical fixes. Not vague advice — specific cues you can use when the coach isn’t there.

04

Supportive club culture

No “elite-only” barrier. Beginners and Kona-level athletes share the same space, with different plans but the same respect.

Meet Coach Fred

Founder & head coach

Frederic Simon

I’ve spent decades in endurance — hundreds of marathons and ultras, 101 self-supported half-Iron distance days in a row, and more time in the pain cave than I care to admit. That experience, plus formal training in coaching, strength & conditioning, and testing, convinced me of one thing: most athletes work way harder than their results suggest. Yousuli exists to fix that gap between effort and outcome.

Certifications: USAT Level II · Ironman certified · TrainingPeaks · Strength & Conditioning · Sport nutrition · Behavior change. Based in Walnut, California — working with athletes worldwide.
