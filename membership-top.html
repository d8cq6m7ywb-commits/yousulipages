The three layers

Stack what you need. Skip what you don’t. Move up when you’re ready.

Layer 01 · Community

Yousuli Social Club

Accessible membership for ordinary humans doing unordinary things. Free or $49/yr with perks. The entry point into the world.

  • Online community + WhatsApp group chat
  • Weekly group training (swim, bike, run)
  • Members-only content: webinars, Q&As, case studies
  • Template plans (first 70.3, first 10K, return from layoff)
  • Member pricing & priority booking on testing

No individual weekly coaching · no daily session feedback · no fully custom programming.

Coaching memberships

Layer 03 · Testing

Performance Lab

Lab-grade testing, on-demand or as part of a structured package. The data that turns coaching into precision.

  • VO₂max, RMR, CdA, swim & gait analysis, full-stack profiling
  • Standalone single tests OR bundled performance packages
  • Member pricing for Club + Coaching athletes
  • Mobile lab covers greater LA + race-week travel

Performance packages: from $300 (Basic) up to $1,500 (Elite Full-Stack). Move up within 60 days, full credit applies.

Help me pick

Five-second answer for the most common situations.

Mainly want community + structure

  • Comfortable making most calls yourself
  • Want others to train with

→ Yousuli Social Club

Want a real plan + regular feedback

  • Better use of every training hour
  • No need for daily contact

→ Structured Coaching ($200/mo)

Big goal + complex constraints

  • Limited time, high target
  • Want someone to own the process

→ Bespoke or Full Performance

Just want the numbers

  • Don’t need ongoing coaching
  • You’ll DIY from data

→ Performance Lab test
