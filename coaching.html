Why Yousuli coaching

Three things that separate coaching with us from a generic training plan.

01

Testing-driven, not template

Plans are built off your real numbers, VO₂max, RMR, CdA, gait, swim metrics. The lab work feeds directly into the coaching, every block.

02

Decision-making, not just delivery

You get a coach for the calls that matter, the workout you should skip, the race to recommit to, the set to push, the week to recover. That’s the work.

03

Efficiency over volume

The aim isn’t more training. It’s more speed per hour you train. Technique, drag, fueling, recovery, the leaks fixed before extra load goes on.

FAQ

What athletes ask before signing up.

Do I need to join the Club to use the Yousuli Lab?

No. The Lab is open to non-members. Club and coaching athletes get member pricing and priority booking, but anyone can book testing directly.

Can I be in the Club without coaching?

Yes. Many athletes start with Club membership only and add coaching or testing later when the timing makes sense.

Can I upgrade from Club to Coaching?

Yes. You can move into Structured or Bespoke Coaching at any time, subject to available coaching slots. We keep the roster small on purpose.

Do you coach beginners?

Yes. The Club and the Basic Foundation package are especially suitable for beginners and returning athletes. Coaching tiers are matched to your current level and goals, not the other way around.

Do you coach only triathletes?

No. We work across triathlon, running, cycling, and swimming. Run-only and bike-only blocks are common. Our calculator library and lab work apply across endurance sports.

Do you offer discounts for para-athletes and elites?

Yes, the Supported Athletes program reserves limited reduced-rate spots for committed para-athletes and elite athletes targeting national or international performance.

Can I move up between coaching tiers?

Yes. If you start at Structured and decide you need more contact, you can move to Bespoke or Full Performance at any time, we credit any unused portion of the current tier toward the upgrade.

Is there a minimum commitment?

For Structured and Bespoke, we typically work in 3-month blocks, long enough to actually see adaptation. Full Performance starts with a quarterly commitment given the testing schedule built in.
