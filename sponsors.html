The first battery-powered, portable swimming machine. Creates stationary swimming in smaller pools so our coaches can observe technique from multiple angles, run drills, and put adult triathletes through serious volume without a full lap pool. 2.5+ miles per battery charge · 1:20/100 yd top pace.
