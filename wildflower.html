VO2 Max Test

$100Wildflower price · normally $160

Maximal graded exercise test with gas exchange analysis. Measures your VO2max, anaerobic threshold, and energy expenditure at each intensity level, and delivers personalized training zones based on heart rate and power.

Duration
~45 min test + ~15 min debrief (~1 hour)
Equipment
VO2Master metabolic analyzer + bike trainer + power & HR

VO2max · anaerobic threshold · energy cost / intensity · HR & power zones

Zoning Test

$40

Heart-rate and power-based assessment to establish your aerobic and anaerobic thresholds and personalized training zones. No gas exchange measurement, focused on delivering accurate zones at a lower price point.

Duration
~45 min test + debrief
Equipment
Bike trainer + heart-rate + power meter
You get
Aerobic & anaerobic thresholds · personalized HR & power zones · race pacing guidance
