How we operate

Six principles. They show up in every service, every plan, every conversation.

01

Test. Don’t guess.

Wearables and formulas estimate. Lab-grade testing measures. Decisions about training, fueling, and equipment should sit on real data — not vibes or someone else’s chart.

02

Efficiency is the shortest path to speed.

Stop wasting effort first. Stop leaving free speed on the table. Add training load only after the leaks are fixed — technique, drag, economy, fueling.

03

Community over ego.

Beginners and high-level athletes share the same environment. Different plans, equal respect. The fast-or-out culture isn’t welcome here.

04

Accessible. Not elite-only.

Endurance sport gets expensive fast. We reserve supported coaching spots for elite and para-athletes who couldn’t otherwise carry the cost — and run partner days that bring real testing to facilities at their members’ price points.

05

Long projects beat quick fixes.

Twelve-week blocks, retests, real adaptation. The 101-day half-Ironman streak wasn’t a stunt — it was a method. Big things are won by showing up across months and years, not days.

06

Science translated to action.

The point isn’t to collect data. It’s to turn it into clearer decisions on training, technique, pacing, and setup — with cues simple enough that you can use them when the coach isn’t there.

The standard

What we expect of athletes — and what athletes can expect of us.

If you work with us

What we ask of you

  • Show up consistently. Plans only work when athletes execute.
  • Provide data honestly — sleep, fueling, life stress, the workout that fell apart.
  • Ask. The questions are part of the work, not a sign you’re behind.
  • Commit to the medium term. Twelve weeks minimum to see real change.
  • Bring your own version of “epic”. We don’t pick it for you.
If we work with you

What you get from us

  • Real attention. Limited coaching roster on purpose.
  • Plans built around your life — not a generic template.
  • Testing that ends in actionable decisions, not data theatre.
  • Clear feedback when something isn’t working — including from us.
  • A community that won’t make you feel small for being new.

Pick your version of epic.

Maybe it’s your first 5 km. Maybe it’s a Kona slot. Maybe it’s swimming continuously for the first time at age 60. The community is built around all of these — not just the loud ones.
