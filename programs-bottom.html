Common program questions

Are programs the same as coaching?

No. Programs teach a topic. Coaching applies it to your specific situation, week by week, with weekly contact and accountability. Many athletes do both, the program teaches the principle, coaching enforces the practice.

Lifetime access, really?

Yes. One purchase, no recurring charges, the program stays in your account. If we update content (we do), updates land in your version automatically.

Can I get a refund?

14-day money back, no questions, on any paid program. We’d rather you opt out cleanly than feel stuck.

Free funnels, what’s the catch?

None. Free programs are real programs with the same standards. We use them as the entry point because the easiest way to know if Yousuli’s teaching style works for you is to actually take some.

Are programs included in coaching?

Coaching athletes get free access to most paid programs as part of their tier. If a program is directly relevant to your training block, your coach will assign it.

I’m not a triathlete, are these for me?

Many programs are sport-agnostic (physiology, fueling, testing literacy). Triathlon-specific ones are flagged in the catalogue. Run-only and bike-only athletes use the calculators & programs heavily.
