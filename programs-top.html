How a program works

Buy → learn → apply — and we’re here if you get stuck.

01

Pick a program

Browse the catalogue below. Free funnels (start there if you’re new), single-topic deep-dives, or full curricula. Each program states what you’ll be able to do by the end.

02

Enroll

Free programs — sign up with email, start in a minute. Paid programs — one-time purchase, lifetime access, no subscription. Account ties to your Yousuli login.

03

Work through it at your pace

Modules in order, or skip around. Each lesson has a short worksheet to make it concrete. Companion calculator links open in a new tab so you can apply numbers without losing your place.

04

Get stuck? Email us.

Programs aren’t coaching, but if you hit a wall on the material we’ll point you at the right answer. Email info@yousuli.co with the program name and lesson number.
